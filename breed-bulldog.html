Bulldog

Country of Origin: United Kingdom

Bulldogs are iconic for their wrinkled faces, stocky build, and endearing personalities. Originally bred for bull-baiting in England, the Bulldog has undergone a transformation into a gentle and affectionate companion. Despite their rugged appearance, Bulldogs are loving, patient, and particularly good with children, making them excellent family pets. Bulldogs are low-energy dogs, well-suited for apartment living or homes with limited space. They enjoy lounging but benefit from moderate exercise to maintain their weight and health.

Energy Level: Low; enjoys short walks and lots of relaxation

Special Care: Requires attention to breathing health and skin folds

Ideal Living Conditions: Adaptable to small spaces, thrives indoors

Size: Medium

Personality: Loyal, courageous, calm

Life Expectancy: 8-12 years
