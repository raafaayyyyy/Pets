Poodle

Country of Origin: Germany (closely associated with France)

Poodles are elegant, intelligent, and active dogs known for their distinctive curly coats. Initially bred in Germany as water retrievers, Poodles quickly gained popularity in France as a symbol of sophistication. They come in three sizes—standard, miniature, and toy—each retaining the breed’s core characteristics of intelligence and energy. Poodles are highly trainable and excel in obedience, agility, and even creative performances. Their hypoallergenic coat makes them an excellent choice for allergy-prone owners, though it requires regular grooming to prevent tangling and matting. Poodles are playful and love human interaction, making them wonderful companions for both families and individuals.

Energy Level: Moderate to high; enjoys exercise and play

Special Care: Regular grooming for their coat

Special Skills: Excels in training, agility, and obedience competitions

Size: Toy, Miniature, Standard

Personality: Intelligent, active, playful

Life Expectancy: 12-15 years
