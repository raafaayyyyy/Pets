Beagle

Country of Origin: United Kingdom

Beagles are small, sturdy hound dogs originally bred for hunting small game such as rabbits. Their keen sense of smell and boundless energy made them ideal hunting companions, and they are still widely used for this purpose today. Their playful and affectionate nature, combined with their compact size, makes them great family pets. Beagles are curious and adventurous, with a love for exploration. Size: Small to medium

Personality: Friendly, curious, merry

Life Expectancy: 12-15 years
